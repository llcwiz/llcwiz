LLCWIZ.com is an innovative online portal designed to make the process of forming and running a limited liability company (LLC) informative and cost effective for aspiring entrepreneurs. With a commitment to transparency and simplicity and flexible DIY options, LLCWIZ.com offers an easy-to-use wizard calculator that provides upfront all-inclusive cost estimates, ensuring you know exactly what you're paying for, to whom you money goes, and what your expected recurring obligations will be.

At the heart of LLCWIZ.com's services is a comprehensive compliance package that caters to all your basic business needs in one convenient interface. Whether you require our basic LLC formation and Registered Agent service, or our full suite including obtaining a Federal Employer Identification Number (FEIN), securing a business license, DBA (doing business as) aliases, and registering for sales tax, LLCWIZ.com streamlines the entire process through a single portal and a one-time payment.

But LLCWIZ.com goes beyond just compliance. The platform also offers a range of technology services to support your business growth. From domain registration and SSL certificates to DNS configuration, email services, mailing services, and hosting solutions, LLCWIZ.com ensures that your online presence and digital infrastructure are secure and optimized for success.

LLCWIZ.com was born out of a frustrating personal experience from using a well-marketed service provider with a confusing lack of transparency, exorbitant recurring charges, and very difficult cancellation procedures. Recognizing the need for a better way to help new entrepreneurs, LLCWIZ.com was founded with the mission of empowering business owners through a detailed pricing structure by entity, DIY options and assistance, full disclosure of estimated recurring charges, and a commitment to exceptional customer service.

With LLCWIZ.com, you can rest assured that you're working with a trusted partner and supporting infrastructure that prioritizes your best interests. The platform's intuitive wizard calculator, much easier to use than a spreadsheet, guides you through the decision-making process, enabling you to choose the perfect combination of DIY and full-service solutions tailored to your specific needs.

If you're just starting out, LLCWIZ.com is the ultimate one-stop-shop for all your LLC formation and technology needs.
